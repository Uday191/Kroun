Most asked questions for intrerviews 

How do you handle a situation where you disagree with your supervisor's decision?

If I disagree with my supervisor's decision, I would respectfully express my concerns and provide any relevant information or alternative solutions that I believe could benefit the situation. However, ultimately, I understand that my supervisor has the authority to make decisions, and I would support their decision and work to implement it effectively.

Would you lie for the company?

It totally depends upon the situation. If lying is the last option to tackle the situation for my company I have to do it but only if it is the last, otherwise I will use my creativity to deal with the situation because in life we always have the second option. We just need to find it and make it count.
